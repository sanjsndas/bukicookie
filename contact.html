Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit my own cookie recipe to BukiCookie?

We love receiving recipe submissions from our community! To submit your original cookie recipe, please use our "Submit a Recipe" form on the website. Include all ingredients with precise measurements, step-by-step instructions, baking times and temperatures, and any special notes. Our editorial team reviews all submissions and may contact you for clarifications before publishing. If your recipe is selected, we'll credit you as the creator and may feature your profile on our site.

What should I do if a recipe isn't working for me?

Baking can sometimes be tricky due to variations in ingredients, altitude, humidity, or equipment. First, double-check that you've followed all steps correctly and measured ingredients precisely. Many issues can be resolved by ensuring your oven temperature is accurate (consider using an oven thermometer). If you're still having trouble, visit the recipe's comment section where our community often shares helpful tips. For persistent issues, contact our recipe support team with details about the problem, and we'll work with our test kitchen to investigate.

How can I convert measurements from metric to imperial or vice versa?

All our recipes include both metric and imperial measurements. If you need to convert between the two, we recommend using our built-in conversion tool on each recipe page. For manual conversions, remember that 1 cup equals 240ml, 1 tablespoon is 15ml, and 1 teaspoon is 5ml. For weight conversions, 1 ounce is approximately 28 grams. However, for baking accuracy, we strongly recommend using a digital kitchen scale and sticking to one measurement system throughout a recipe rather than converting mid-recipe.

Do you offer nutrition information for your recipes?

Yes, we provide estimated nutritional information for most of our recipes, including calories, macronutrients, and common allergens. This information is generated using industry-standard calculation methods and database references. However, these are estimates only, as actual values can vary based on specific ingredient brands, exact measurements, and preparation methods. For medical dietary needs, we recommend consulting a nutrition professional. You can find the nutrition information at the bottom of each recipe card.

Can I request a specific type of cookie recipe?

Absolutely! We welcome recipe requests from our community. Use our "Recipe Request" form to tell us about the type of cookie you're looking for - whether it's a traditional recipe from a specific culture, a dietary-specific version (like gluten-free or vegan), or a creative flavor combination. Our recipe development team considers all requests when planning new content. While we can't fulfill every individual request, popular suggestions often become future featured recipes. You can also check our existing collection using our advanced search filters.

40 Ways to Connect With BukiCookie

Recipe Development Inquiries

Professional bakers and food scientists interested in collaborating on recipe development can reach our culinary team at recipes@bukicookie.com. Include your background, area of expertise, and samples of previous work. We're particularly interested in authentic regional cookie recipes and innovative dietary-specific adaptations.

Food Photography Submissions

Photographers can submit cookie photography for potential features on our site and social media. Email samples to photos@bukicookie.com with subject line "Photo Submission". Include your Instagram handle if applicable. We credit all photographers and may offer paid assignments for particularly strong portfolios.

Media & Press Requests

Journalists and media professionals should contact press@bukicookie.com for interviews, expert commentary, or story collaborations. Our team can provide insights on baking trends, holiday cookie data, and cultural cookie traditions. Please allow 3-5 business days for response to media inquiries.

Retail & Product Partnerships

For inquiries about branded baking products, cookbook collaborations, or retail partnerships, email business@bukicookie.com. We're open to discussions about co-branded baking mixes, tools, and exclusive product lines that align with our commitment to quality and innovation in home baking.

Baking Class Instructors

Professional bakers interested in teaching virtual or in-person classes through BukiCookie should send resumes and class proposals to education@bukicookie.com. We're looking for instructors specializing in various techniques, from beginner basics to advanced decorating skills.

International Correspondents

We're building a network of international contributors to document authentic cookie traditions worldwide. If you can provide insights about your country's baking heritage, email world@bukicookie.com with your location and area of knowledge. Compensation varies by assignment.

Influencer Collaborations

Content creators with engaged baking audiences can inquire about sponsorship opportunities at influencers@bukicookie.com. Include your platform stats, audience demographics, and examples of previous branded content. We prioritize authentic, skilled bakers who align with our brand values.

Charity & Community Baking

For organizations seeking baking-related donations or partnerships for charitable causes, contact giving@bukicookie.com. We regularly support baking education programs, food banks, and community initiatives through recipe donations, baking kits, and volunteer participation.

Science of Baking Questions

Our resident food scientist answers technical baking questions at science@bukicookie.com. Whether you're curious about the chemistry of leaveners or how altitude affects baking times, we'll provide detailed explanations to deepen your understanding of baking science.

Kids Baking Programs

Educators and parents can access our free kids' baking resources at kids@bukicookie.com. We provide age-appropriate recipes, safety guides, and lesson plans designed to teach children baking fundamentals while fostering creativity and kitchen confidence.
