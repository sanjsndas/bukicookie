Cookie Policy

Comprehensive Cookie Policy for BukiCookie

Last Updated: January 1, 2023

This Cookie Policy explains how BukiCookie ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website. By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer, smartphone, or other device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, provide reporting information, and assist with website personalization.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes including:

  • Essential operation of our website
  • Performance monitoring and analytics
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Advertising and marketing purposes
  • Social media integration

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for you to browse the website and use its features. Without these cookies, services like shopping baskets or e-billing cannot be provided.

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that remain in your browser until you leave the site.
  • Authentication Cookies: Used to identify you when you log into our site.
  • Security Cookies: Help prevent fraudulent use of login credentials.

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies collect information about how visitors use our website to help us improve how our website works.

  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.
  • Error Management Cookies: Help us measure errors that occur on the website.
  • Testing Cookies: Used when we're testing new features or designs.

3.3 Functionality Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make to give you better functionality and personal features.

  • Preference Cookies: Remember your preferences like language or region.
  • Feature Cookies: Enable additional features like remembering login details.
  • Social Media Cookies: Allow integration with social platforms.

3.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver ads more relevant to you and your interests.

  • First-party Advertising Cookies: For our own marketing purposes.
  • Third-party Advertising Cookies: Placed by advertising networks.
  • Retargeting Cookies: Help show relevant ads based on your browsing.

4. Detailed Contact-Related Points Regarding Cookies

4.1 Contact for Cookie Preferences

You can contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@bukicookie.com to discuss your cookie preferences. Our team will guide you through the process of adjusting your settings and explain how different cookies affect your experience. We typically respond within 48 hours during business days.

4.2 Reporting Cookie Issues

If you experience any issues related to cookies (such as errors or malfunctions), please contact support@bukicookie.com with details of the problem. Include your browser version, device type, and screenshots if possible to help us resolve the issue faster.

4.3 Third-Party Cookie Inquiries

For questions about third-party cookies used on our site, contact thirdparty@bukicookie.com. We maintain a list of all third-party services that place cookies through our website and can provide details about their data collection practices.

4.4 Legal Inquiries About Cookies

For legal questions regarding our cookie usage, contact legal@bukicookie.com. Our legal team can provide information about compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations as they relate to our cookie practices.

4.5 Technical Support for Cookie Management

Our technical support team can be reached at techsupport@bukicookie.com for help with managing cookies in your browser. They can provide step-by-step guidance for different browsers and devices to help you control cookie settings.

4.6 Marketing Cookie Opt-Out

To opt out of marketing cookies specifically, email marketing@bukicookie.com with "Marketing Cookie Opt-Out" in the subject line. Please note this may affect the relevance of ads you see but won't reduce the number of ads displayed.

4.7 Cookie Consent Withdrawal

You can withdraw your cookie consent at any time by contacting consent@bukicookie.com. This will initiate the process of removing non-essential cookies from your device when you next visit our site.

4.8 Accessibility Concerns With Cookie Banner

If you have difficulty accessing or interacting with our cookie consent banner due to accessibility issues, please contact accessibility@bukicookie.com. We're committed to making our cookie management tools available to all users.

4.9 Business Hours for Cookie-Related Inquiries

4.11 Data Subject Requests Regarding Cookies

4.12 Cookie-Related Complaints

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed.

5.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.aboutcookies.org.

5.2 Opt-Out Tools

For third-party advertising cookies, you can opt out through:

  • Digital Advertising Alliance's opt-out page: optout.aboutads.info
  • European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance: www.youronlinechoices.com
  • Network Advertising Initiative: optout.networkadvertising.org
